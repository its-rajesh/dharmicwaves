Karna vs Arjuna: What the Mahabharata Really Teaches About Truth and Dharma

Rajesh published on 2025-06-17
Setting the Stage We all know that the Bhagavad Gita was heard by two people: Arjuna, and Dhritarashtra—through the words of Sanjaya. One transformed his life with that knowledge, understanding the complexities of existence and the way of dharma. The other… simply thought Krishna was brainwashing Arjuna. What does this tell us? That knowledge, however divine, depends entirely on the receiver. It’s not the clarity of the message that matters—it’s the clarity of the mind that hears it.
